Defining Web Serial

Web serials are episodic writings that are posted online for readers to enjoy. They come in a chapter at a time, usually on a set schedule, and are just about the most accessible form of publishing available to hopeful writers. They also provide quick and easy reading for readers who are willing to accept less editing polish in exchange for free content.
