Where to shop sustainably

The first step to committing to be earth-friendly is ditching big corporations as much as you can. When you shop at places like Amazon, Target, Walmart, Trader Joe's, and Costco, you are supporting poorer working conditions, often unethical treatment of animals, high carbon emmissions, and more. Shopping small at businesses that support environmentalism not only eases your conscience but also helps the planet in a big way. Take it from us, the first step is to start finding good websites that focus on reducing our carbon footprint while keeping prices competitive with large corporations.
