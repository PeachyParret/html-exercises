30 Minute or Less Recipes

Chicken Stir Fry

This recipe is a great one to check out! There are multiple variations that I like to do to this. One thing that I do to make it faster is buying frozen vegetables. All you need is chicken breast, Minute Rice, vegetables, and a sauce of your choice. Sometimes I get a bag of frozen chicken strips (healhty ones) to use so I don't need to cook chicken breasts.

  • Prepare the chicken in whatever way you prefer
  • Cook the frozen vegetables and rice at the same time
  • All three foods should be done at the same time and enjoy!

Chicken Salad

All you need for this recipe are fresh vegetables, salad, chicken, and a dressing of your choice. I like to add rice and hummus into my salad as well to add a little extra flavor!

  • Prepare the chicken in whatever way you prefer
  • Cut up the vegetables while the chicken is cooking
  • Add your toppings and enjoy!

Stuffed chicken Breast

All you need for this recipe is a chicekn breast, italian seasoning, bell peppers, greek yogurt, an onion, and spinach. You can serve it on a salad or on top of rice. This recipe is for four chicken breasts.

  • In a bowl, mix 1/2 cup of greek yogurt, 2 cups of spinach (heated), 1 chopped bell pepper, and 1/4 an onion
  • Make a slit into each of the chicken breast
  • Season the chicken with salt, pepper, and italian seasoning
  • Stuff the ingrediants from the bowl into each of the chicken breasts
  • Bake at 400 degrees farenhiet for 25 minutes

Turkey Burrito Bowl

This recipe is very versitaile. I like to make a lot of it and eat it with several different sides throughout the week. Try putting it on salads, in a tortilla, or plain with a side of vegetables.

  • Sautee 1/4 of an onion and 1 bell pepper over medium heat
  • Add in 1 pound of ground turkey and 2 tablespoons of taco seasoning
  • Once throughly cooked, add in 1/2 cup of salsa
