About the Swim School

The Swim School was created in order to foster a love for the water and raise awareness about the risks of water safety in the community. We aim to achieve this goal by creating a safe, and caring atmosphere where parents and children can begin to become comfortable with the water.

About the Instructors

Joseph: Joseph is the founder of our Swim School, and he acts as a subsititute for any emergencies. Joseph founded the school because as a child he nearly drowned at a friends birthday party, and the next week he was placed into swim lessons by his parents. It started a life long love of the water, where he eventually became a national champion in college and figured the best way to continue his passion would be to share it qwith others.

Dylan: Dylan was a college swimmer who wanted to be able to stay involved in the sport, and enjoys working with kids, so that lead him to us. He loves watching the children learn and develop a healthy relationship with the water.

Lauren: Lauren was also a college swimmer, who majored in early development. She worked at a local hosipital for a few years, but ended up missing the water, and teaching children is the best of both worlds.

Class Options

Here at the swim school we offer a range of options so you can pick what best suits your family.

Parent and child

This class is designed for infants and their parents, so both family memebers can overcome any worry the water and begin to become comfortable.

Toddlers

This is designed toddlers and spends much of the class on guided play, which allows the children to have fun, without realzing that they learning the basics of water safety.

Juniors

Juniors is designed to give children who may not have learned to swim at a young age or are not comfortable being in the water a safe place to learn and start the basics of the four different strokes.
