Class Options

Here at the swim school we offer a range of options so you can pick what best suits your family.

Parent and child

This class is designed for infants and their parents, so both family memebers can overcome any worry the water and begin to become comfortable.

Toddlers

This is designed toddlers and spends much of the class on guided play, which allows the children to have fun, without realzing that they learning the basics of water safety.

Juniors

Juniors is designed to give children who may not have learned to swim at a young age or are not comfortable being in the water a safe place to learn and start the basics of the four different strokes.
