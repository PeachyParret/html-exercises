Tips and Tricks

Canva has a premium subscription service where you can pay money for "better" or "more aesthetic" elements and templates. However, you shouldn't have to pay to create the designs you want. The best thing about Canva is that you have the tools to create whatever you want. Most of what you pay for with Canva Pro is just the labor of someone else making the templates or uploading assets. If you're willing to put in the work, you can make anything you want. (All of the examples on this page were made with the free version of Canva!)
