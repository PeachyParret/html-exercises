    • The Do's

    • Plant seeds immediately

      Mayor Lewis gifts you 15 parsnips seeds your first day. Using your hoe and watering can, plant them as soon as you walk outside. When you get more money, immediately go buy more seeds at Pierre's.

    • Check and open your mailbox every morning

      Villagers send you useful information, gifts and even money. Make sure to look for the mail icon above the mailbox every morning.

    • Meet and befriend every villager

      Villagers often gift you useful resources if you have a high relationship. Make sure to gve gifts and talk to villagers daily. You can also romance and marry single villagers.

    • Complete the Community Center ASAP!!

      During the first few days. Mayor Lewis will tell you about the abandoned community center. Make sure to complete bundles to unlock it as soon as you can. It fixes broken items around the village and boosts friendships.

    • Don't be afraid to spend your money early

      The more seeds and livestock you buy, the more money will get. Don't get so hungup on saving when you can earned four times more.

    • Level up your skills quickly

      If you have spare time in the day, use your remaining energy to level up skills. Tasks become easier and you gain helpful perks.

    • Begin to organize chests early on in the game

      Color-coding chests is a good way to make gameplay easier and limit wasted time.

    • Begin to organize chests early on in the game

      Though it can seem intimidating, mining is a great way to progress the game and acquire needed materials for tool upgrades. Winter is a good time to mine constantly.

    • Upgrade your tools ASAP (but only certain ones)

      Your pickaxe, axe, watering can and hoe are vital to upgrade as soon as possible. It makes tasks much easier and saves you a lot of energy. Be aware that it takes one or multiple days for your tool to be done.

    The Don'ts
    • Do not sell everything right away!
      • Having one of everything (foragables, crops, ore, etc.) is a good way to stay on top of the community center and any other requests from villagers.
    • Do not forget to water your crops
      • Missing even one day of watering can hold your plant back one day of growing. This can really hurt you come the end of the season.
    • Do not plant your plants at the end of the season (or when you don't have enough days left in the season for that specific crop)
      • Every plant has a growth time. Make sure you take not of how long each plant takes to grow and plan accordingly.
    • Do not cut all your grass or chop down all trees at one time
      • Crafting grass requires a lot of fiber, fiber much needed for other means of crafting. You can buy grass from Pierre, but it is expensive early on. If you do not have grass for your animals, they cannot eat and you have buy hay. Cut grass as needed. Trees take a lot of time to grow back. Wood is easy to collect, so don't go crazy and leave yourself with no wood for days.
    • Do no put off the community center
      • Keep in mind items you need for the community center. Don't think you can just wait to do it, unless you plan on buying th Joja Mart membership ...traitor.
    • Do not go into the mine unprepared
      • Make sure whenever you make a trip to the mine, you have the necessary tools, food, and health tonics to stay alive and swift.
