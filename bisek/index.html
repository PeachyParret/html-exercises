    • The Do's

    • Plant seeds immediately!!
      • Mayor Lewis gifts you 15 parsnips seeds your first day. Using your hoe and watering can, plant them as soon as you walk outside. When you get more money, immediately go buy more seeds at Pierre's.
    • Check and open your mailbox every morning
      • Villagers send you useful information, gifts and even money. Make sure to look for the mail icon above the mailbox every morning.
    • Meet and befriend every villager
      • Villagers often gift you useful resources if you have a high relationship. Make sure to gift and talk to villagers daily. You can also romance and marry single villagers.
    • Complete the Community Center ASAP!
      • During the first few days. Mayor Lewis will tell you about the abandoned community center. Make sure to complete bundles to unlock it as soon as you can. It fixes broken items around the village and boosts friendships.
    • Don't be afraid to spend your money early in the game
      • The more seeds and livestock you buy, the more money will get. Don't get so hungup on saving when you can earned four times more.
    • Level up your skills quickly
      • If you have spare time in the day, use your remaining energy to level up skills. Tasks become easier and you gain helpful perks.
    • Begin to organize chests early on
      • Color-coding chests is a good way to make gameplay easier and limit wasted time.
    • MINE, MINE, MINE!
      • Though it can seem intimidating, mining is a great way to progress the game and acquire needed materialas for tool upgrades. Winter is a good time to mine constantly.
    • Upgrade tools ASAP (but only certain ones)
      • Your pickaxe, axe, watering can and hoe are vital to upgrade as soon as possible. It makes tasks much easier and saves you a lot of energy. Be aware that it takes one or multiple days for your tool to be done.
